Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Image Compressor from MB to kB. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle, protect, and use your personal information when you use our online tool to reduce image sizes. Our service is compatible with JPG, PNG, and WebP formats and is committed to respecting your privacy rights. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we manage your data.

Information We Collect

Our image compression tool does not require users to provide any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers to use the core compression features. However, if you choose to contact us through the website’s contact form or subscribe to newsletters, we may collect the following information:

  • Name: Collected if you submit your details via a contact form.
  • Email address: Collected if you wish to receive responses or subscribe to updates.
  • Any messages you provide: Such as support inquiries, feedback, or requests for assistance.

This information will only be used to respond to your inquiries or send updates you’ve opted into.

Image Data Handling

When you upload images to our tool, the images are temporarily stored on our servers for the purpose of compression. We do not retain or store your images after the compression process is complete. The images are automatically deleted from our system after processing, ensuring that no image data is stored, reused, or shared. This process happens in real-time and is crucial to maintaining your privacy.

We do not inspect, modify, or analyze your images for any purpose other than compression. Your images remain private, and we have no access to the image contents beyond the compression function.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and improve the functionality of our services. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that allow us to recognize you on subsequent visits and remember your preferences. They help us gather anonymous data about site usage, which enables us to optimize the platform’s performance.

We may use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the basic operation of the website, such as page navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect information about how visitors interact with the site, enabling us to improve functionality and user experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand site traffic and user behavior, often provided by third-party analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics).

You have the option to disable cookies in your browser settings. However, doing so may limit certain functionalities of our site.

We may partner with third-party service providers, such as analytics platforms, to better understand how users interact with our website and improve its performance. These third parties may collect data such as:

This information is collected and used in aggregate form, meaning it is anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual users. We only use this data to analyze trends, monitor site usage, and improve the functionality and user experience of the tool.
